              wreckage of a ship or its cargo found floating on or washed up by the sea

            "He evinced trepidation at the upcoming task"

"My political weltanschauung is very different from most of my family's."

                "he is the supernal poet of our age"

                 "he was a vociferous opponent of the takeover"

                "a maverick among Connecticut Republicans"
